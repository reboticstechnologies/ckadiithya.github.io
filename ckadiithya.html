CK Adiithya - Project Information

CK Adiithya

Plotted development located on Bannerghatta–Jigani–Anekal Main Road (Indalwadi Cross). Developed by M/s. Prajwal Promoters Private Limited, CK Adiithya is an 11-acre themed residential layout with extensive amenities and RERA approval.

Overview

CK Adiithya is a plotted development designed to offer landscaped living, recreational amenities and modern infrastructure. The project emphasizes green spaces, jogging tracks, water features, and community facilities to promote a healthy lifestyle.

Promoter & Background

The project is promoted by M/s. Prajwal Promoters Private Limited. The promoter group has a long history in real estate development and construction, bringing decades of experience to CK Adiithya. (Source documents provided.)

Key Amenities

  • Club House and Swimming Pool
  • Basketball and Tennis Courts
  • Children’s Play Area and Meditation Pavilion
  • Landscape gardens with water bodies and lakes
  • Jogging track, yoga areas and dedicated open spaces
  • Underground/overhead water storage and standby power for common areas

Project USP

  • First project approved by STRRPA in the area
  • Vastu-compliant land parcels
  • Plotted development offering villa and row-house options
  • Emphasis on eco-friendly features such as rainwater harvesting and recycling

Approvals & Compliance

CK Adiithya holds multiple statutory approvals including BMRDA, KSPCB, BESCOM, KIADB, KHB, and RERA (Acknowledgement No. PR/KN/170803/001055). The project approval date recorded in the RERA certificate is 19 October 2017. fileciteturn0file1
