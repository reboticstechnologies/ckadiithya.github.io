Departments & Committees

Below is the list of departments and committees where volunteers are welcome. Click any department to view details and volunteering tasks.

Administration & HR Department

Manage staff, office coordination, and day-to-day operations of the association.

Finance & Accounts Department

Handle budgeting, accounts, maintenance fee tracking, and financial reporting.

Maintenance & Infrastructure Department

Oversee roads, lighting, plumbing, drainage, and layout repairs.

Security & Safety Committee

Manage security personnel, CCTV planning, and safety initiatives.

Cultural & Events Committee

Plan and organize community functions, festivals and cultural programs.

Sports & Recreation Committee

Conduct sports tournaments, tournaments and fitness activities.

Pooja & Temple Committee

Organize and manage religious/spiritual events and temple upkeep.

Water Management & Utility Committee

Monitor borewells, water supply, tank maintenance and utilities.

Garden & Beautification Committee

Maintain greenery, parks, planting drives and landscaping.

Waste Management & Sanitation Department

Promote cleanliness, waste segregation and sanitation drives.

Communication & IT Department

Manage website, newsletters, digital platforms and resident databases.

Legal & Compliance Department

Handle documentation, RERA matters, statutory compliance and legal support.

Membership & Public Relations Department

Manage resident registrations, outreach and engagement.

Development & Planning Committee

Propose and oversee layout improvement and infrastructure projects.

Grievance Redressal & Welfare Committee

Address resident complaints and ensure welfare and relief.

Education & Youth Committee

Organize children's programs, tuition support and youth activities.

Senior Citizens & Welfare Committee

Plan activities and support for senior residents.

Women's Empowerment Committee

Encourage women-led initiatives, awareness and training programs.

Disaster Management & Emergency Response Committee

Prepare safety plans, first-response teams and emergency drills.

Audit & Compliance Sub-Committee

Review financial and operational accountability and audits.
