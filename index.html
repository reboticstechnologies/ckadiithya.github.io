Welcome to CK Adiithya Layout Residents Association

Working together for a safer, cleaner and connected neighbourhood. Registered as CKALRA — representing residents of CK Adiithya Layout.

Panchayat Liaison

Representation in Panchayat meetings for handover, services and approvals.

Service Coordination

Security, water, waste management: coordinate vendors and handovers.

Community Events

Meetings, notices and programs to keep the layout connected.

Departments in Association

1. Administration & HR Department - Manage staff, office coordination, and operations. 2. Finance & Accounts Department - Handle budgeting, accounts, and maintenance fee tracking. 3. Maintenance & Infrastructure Department - Oversee roads, lighting, plumbing, and layout repairs. 4. Security & Safety Committee - Manage security personnel and layout safety initiatives. 5. Cultural & Events Committee - Plan and organize community functions and festivals. 6. Sports & Recreation Committee - Conduct sports tournaments and fitness activities. 7. Pooja & Temple Committee - Organize and manage religious or spiritual events. 8. Water Management & Utility Committee - Monitor borewells, water supply, and utilities. 9. Garden & Beautification Committee - Maintain greenery, parks, and planting drives. 10. Waste Management & Sanitation Department - Promote cleanliness and waste segregation. 11. Communication & IT Department - Manage website, newsletters, and digital platforms. 12. Legal & Compliance Department - Handle documentation, RERA, and statutory compliance. 13. Membership & Public Relations Department - Manage resident registrations and engagement. 14. Development & Planning Committee - Suggest and oversee layout improvement projects. 15. Grievance Redressal & Welfare Committee - Address complaints and ensure resident welfare. 16. Education & Youth Committee - Conduct children's programs and educational initiatives. 17. Senior Citizens & Welfare Committee - Plan activities and support for senior members. 18. Women's Empowerment Committee - Encourage women-led initiatives and awareness programs. 19. Disaster Management & Emergency Response Committee - Prepare safety and response plans. 20. Audit & Compliance Sub-Committee - Review financial and operational accountability..
