About CK Adiithya Layout Residents Association

CK Adiithya Layout Residents Association (CKALRA) represents the residents of CK Adiithya Layout. Our goal is to ensure transparent handover processes, continuity of essential services, and a well-maintained neighbourhood through community participation and coordination with the Panchayat and relevant authorities.

Our Mission

  • Secure timely handover from the builder to the Panchayat & from Panchayat to Association.
  • Ensure continuous essential services — water, security and waste management.
  • Maintain transparent finances and regular communication with residents.
