Why Become a Member?

CK Adiithya Layout Residents Association (CKALRA) is a registered body of residents formed under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960. Membership enables you to participate in decisions of the layout, vote in association matters, receive official updates and be part of welfare and development initiatives.

  • Official recognition as a member of CKALRA.
  • Right to participate and vote in General Body meetings and elections.
  • Access to certified communication, notices and meeting minutes.
  • Participation in committees (maintenance, culture, cleanliness, security etc.).

After paying the membership fee, residents are requested to share payment acknowledgement along with Name, Address, Mobile Number and Email ID to ckadithyalayout@gmail.com and WhatsApp +91-9686847093 for issuance of the Official Membership Certificate.
